Elections will be generated through a 'Life Event' - this includes Open Enrollment.

* An election during open enrollment ends up in the Enrollment table for an enrollment that is tied to open enrollment.
  So, elections made at other times can end up in the Enrollment table for an enrollment that is tied to a different life event.
* Add an 'exported' column (boolean) indicating whether or not we have sent this info to the carriers.
* Add an 'exported_date' column (datetime) indicating when the exporter picked up this enrollment to send it to the carriers.


Exporter can be called by a celery task that runs nightly/daily/whatever and looks at the Enrollment table for entries where exported == False.  Elections tied to the Enrollment are then grouped by employee and sent to the carrier.

if open enrollment is going on (e.g. an open_start and open_end encompass today's date) then:
  - when an election is made the enrollment will have open enrollment as the life event
otherwise:
  - the life events step will be the only one that is active, once selected the other steps become activated
    elections will then have the life event appropriately set
